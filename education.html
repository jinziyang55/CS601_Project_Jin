My Education Experience

Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics

Because of my enthusiasm in aviation since very young, I studied Air Transport Engineering in Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics during 2012-2016. The main courses are Aircraft System, Aviation Chart, Aeronautical Meterology, Flight Performance Engineering, Radar and Communication Navagation. I learned the aircarft principles and flight operation basis knowledge. I feel it is my life-long passion to pursue.

Boston University

Due to the Covid, we have to suspend most of our international flights including all the flights to United States. To take advantage of this period of time, I enrolled Software Development Program in Boston Unversity in 2020 Fall. I learned Python, Java, SQL, Data Structures and Algorithms, Design Patterns, Artificial Intelligence and Web Application Development. I am expected to graduate in January 2022 and hope to work as a front-end web and mobile developer in the future.

My Work Experience

After I graduated from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics in 2016, I joined Hainan Airlines, a Skytrax 5-star airline. Below is a video description about our distinctive Kungfu Panda Dreamliner Boeing 787-9.



I have worked as Flight Controller and Station Operation Manager in several cities around the globe.

  1. Flight Controller (2016-2017) Beijing, China
    My main responsibilies are as below.
    • Exercise the operational control on a co-authority basis with the Pilot-In-Command and for the joint responsibility of the safe and efficient planning and monitoring of a flight.
    • Issue the Operational Flight Plans in accordance with the Aircraft Operating Manual, Flight Operations Manual and governing regulations.
    • Perform the Flight Watch, analyse operational conditions and identify any opportunities that may impact operational capabilities, including weather, Air Traffic Control, aircraft performance, airports, runways, airways and navigational aids.

  2. Station Operation Manager (2018-Now) Sydney/Cairns, Australia; Las Vegas/Los Angeles/Boston, United States
    My main responsibilies are as below.
    • Supervise the overall management and assignment of flights to be operated including GHA functions, key performance indicator, training and development. Ensure the ground handling and ramp operation conform to the safety and security standards.
    • Oversee the ramp handling activities in liaison with GHA, Caterers, Fuelling, Maintenance, Cargo, and Crew to expedite departure.
    • Identify and resolve the service and operational issues. Handle the flight disruptions impacted by aircraft swaps, flight delays, cancellations and any other factors may affect the operation.
